About VibeAudio

Browser edition

VibeAudio is being shaped as a browser-first audiobook shelf for listeners who want instant playback, synced progress, and a calmer way to return to long-form stories.

The focus for this version is simple: better discovery, better resume logic, and a player that feels dependable instead of temporary.

Everything on this page is tuned for web listening first, so the browser experience can stand on its own before any app-specific extras.
